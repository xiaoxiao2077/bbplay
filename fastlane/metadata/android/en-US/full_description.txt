BBPlay is a third-party Bilibili player application designed specifically for children, providing a safe and simple video viewing experience. The application supports both mobile and desktop devices, with complete video playback, history, favorites and other functions.

Key Features:
• Video playback with landscape and portrait modes
• History tracking
• Favorites management
• Time slot viewing
• Search functionality
• QR code login
• AI summary of videos
• Subtitle support
• Playback controls
• Multi-platform support (Android, iOS, Windows, macOS)

This app is built with Flutter and is completely open source. It contains no ads or tracking, providing a safe environment for children to enjoy videos.
